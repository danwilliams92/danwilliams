About Me

Work Process

Although my work so far has only manifested in the form of projects (as opposed to real-world websites made for clients), I still try to take methodical steps in the process.

I follow these guidelines when working on my projects:
  • Gather information. Decide what product or service the website wants to offer, who their target audience(s) is/are, and how I can model the website towards these people
  • Look to other functioning websites that are offering a similar service - see what they have going for them and what they're missing or could be doing better (that I could use in my website)
  • Produce physical sketches of how I could present and organise the website in desktop, mobile and tablet formats. Make a note of what features they might use.
  • Get together suitable, good-quality images for the website; select an appropriate typography and colour scheme, as well as appropriate icons if needed
  • Produce the markup, styling and javascript for the website
  • Test the appearance and functionality of the website on various browsers and device dimensions. Ask myself if I can make changes to improve the user's experience
